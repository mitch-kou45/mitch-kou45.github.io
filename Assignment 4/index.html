Sacramento Kings' History

Topic I find interesting:
The Sacramento Kings are a professional basketball team based in Sacramento, California, competing in the Western Conference of the NBA. Established in 1923, the franchise has experienced multiple relocations and name changes throughout its history. Initially known as the Rochester Royals, the team joined the National Basketball League (NBL) in 1945 and won the championship in its debut season. Following the merger of the NBL and the Basketball Association of America (BAA) in 1949, the Royals became part of the NBA and secured their sole NBA championship in 1951. In 1957, the franchise moved to Cincinnati, Ohio. Relocating again in 1972, the team became the Kansas City-Omaha Kings, eventually simplifying its name to Kansas City Kings in 1975. Since moving to Sacramento in 1985, they've had their best season in 2002 reaching the Western conference finals, but then precede to have a playoff drought until recently in the 2022-2023 season becoming third seed.
